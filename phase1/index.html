Our World — Changelog

v2.10.1Jul 11 2026Improve
  • Tap the map to drop a pin when adding a place — no need to know the coordinates
  • The pin shows on a mini map before you save, so you can see it landed in the right spot; typed coordinates preview the same way
  • Out-of-range or half-filled coordinates are caught and explained instead of silently saving a bad pin
  • A Clear button removes the pin — the place then stays in the list but off the map
v2.10.0Jul 11 2026Feature
  • You can now add your own places. Hit + New Place on the Landmarks tab — give it a name, a type, an optional country or state code, and optional coordinates
  • Added places behave like any other: status cycling, dates, search, filters, leaderboard, and bulk add/remove
  • Give it coordinates and it appears as a dot on the world map; leave them blank and it stays in the list only
  • New Mine filter chip shows just your added places, and you can delete them from the same panel
  • Your places sync across devices like everything else
v2.9.0Jul 11 2026Feature
  • Added every U.S. national forest — 118 new entries, bringing the forest list to 134 U.S. units (the full USFS list, counted as 154 when hyphenated names are split) plus 9 international forests
  • Coordinates come from the official U.S. Board on Geographic Names data, so every forest lands in the right place on the map
  • Olympic and Sequoia exist as both a park and a forest — the forests are listed as “Olympic NF” and “Sequoia NF”, and typing the full name resolves to the right one
  • 483 places now on the Landmarks tab
v2.8.2Jul 11 2026Fix
  • Removed the hardcoded Laura + Faron placeholders — the page no longer flashes made-up people while it connects
  • An empty board now shows a “tap + Add” prompt instead of inventing participants, and a first-time setup no longer writes placeholder people into the database
  • If the participants data can’t be read, the app now says so instead of quietly swapping in placeholder names
v2.8.1Jul 11 2026Fix
  • Cards no longer jump when you click them — clicking a status re-sorted the grid immediately, so the card moved out from under you
  • The grid now holds its order while you're clicking and re-sorts 2 seconds after your last click
  • A card you just clicked stays put even if the active filter would hide it, until the re-sort
  • If a save fails or you're offline, the click now visibly reverts instead of showing a status that was never saved
v2.8.0Jul 11 2026Feature
  • One click now marks a place Visited. The status cycle was none → candidate → planned → visited, so it took three clicks to log a visit; it now runs none → visited → planned → candidate → none
  • Added 129 UNESCO World Heritage Sites (Pompeii, Versailles, Petra, Angkor, Machu Picchu, Kyoto, Samarkand, Tikal, and many more)
  • New UNESCO filter chip — it spans every category, so places already listed that are World Heritage (Yellowstone, Banff, Taj Mahal, Butrint…) show up under it too: 193 UNESCO sites in all
  • 365 places now on the Landmarks tab
v2.7.2Jul 11 2026Feature

  • Switzerland: added its 12 regional nature parks (Parc Ela, Val Müstair, Gruyère Pays-d'Enhaut, and more) — the country has just one official national park, already listed
